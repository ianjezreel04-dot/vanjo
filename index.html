About the Platform

A Campus Built to Empower Students

MalahSCI MarketHub was created to give MalahSCI students a dedicated space to showcase what they can offer whether it is a talent, a skill, or a handcrafted product. Staff members and the school community can browse the listings, view student profiles with photos and contact details, and reach out directly. This platform exists to turn campus talent into real opportunities and to help students earn while they learn.

How It Works

Three easy steps to connect with MalahSCI student talent and products

01

Tap & Browse

Open the Skills or Products tab and simply touch/click any card to zoom in and read the full details of what the student has to offer.

02

Find What You Need

Whether you are looking for a dance coach, a coding tutor, a motorcycle repair specialist, or a handmade gift, every listing is verified.

03

Reach Out Directly

Use the contact button inside the zoomed view to reach the student directly on their social media. All arrangements are personal and direct.
